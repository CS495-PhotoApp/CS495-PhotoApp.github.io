PhotoTK is an app that aims to provide photographers with a better way to take pictures with cameras that can include more details in it. The application will give you better support when you want to adjust shutter speed, ISO and aperture, and/or you feel like adding more information (geological location, weather) for your photo. No need to do a lot of research and learn about the camera if you are just a beginner that’s interested in photography, we got them all in this toolkit app for you.
Easy, convenient and quality. Lots of unique, useful features all in one. You don’t have to download many other useless apps anymore. With this app, You can easily take a photo that tells stories.
